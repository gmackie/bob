# ForgeGraph Positioning

**Date:** 2026-06-01
**Status:** Draft

ForgeGraph is positioned as app delivery infrastructure for small teams and agent-assisted contributors who want the deploy loop, work-item state, CI/CD evidence, and runtime ownership in one place without adopting a full Kubernetes platform.
