# Code of Conduct

Bob Builder spaces should stay practical, respectful, and focused on building
reliable self-hosted DevOps tools.

Expected behavior:

- Be direct without being hostile.
- Assume public comments will be read by future contributors.
- Keep criticism tied to code, docs, behavior, or project direction.
- Respect maintainer scope decisions for prototype-stage work.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, personal attacks, sustained
off-topic disruption, spam, and publishing private information.

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; lock threads; or block
participants when needed to keep the project usable. To report a conduct issue,
email the maintainer address listed in `package.json` or open a private security
advisory if the report includes sensitive information.
